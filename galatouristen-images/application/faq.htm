5. When is the best time to travel to Vietnam?

Viet Nam
  • March and April are the hottest and most humid period of time in Ho Chi Minh City and other provinces in the south of Vietnam. The dry season runs from November to April, and the rainy season lasts from May to October. In the north, the wet season is from May to September while the period of time from December to March is the driest of the year.
Cambodia
  • With a tropical climate, Cambodia has three seasons. The most pleasant season, the dry one is from November to February with a cooler average temperature. The hot season lasts from March to May with a high average temperature. The monsoon season, hot and humid, is from June to October. The wettest months of the year are August and September.
Laos
  • The rainy season lasts from May through October with hot and humid weather. The dry season begins in November with a cooler average temperature giving way the hot and dry feeling during April and May.

10. Do I need to have any vaccine shots before the trip?
  • No vaccination is officially required by Vietnamese authorities, however immunization against cholera, hepatitis, typhoid, tetanus, polio and Japanese encephalitis is advised. Please consult your doctors for further medical advice. Bringing mosquito repellent, wearing long-sleeved shirts & slacks and avoiding perfume are also recommended. In addition to an ample supply of your prescribed drugs (and the prescription which can be filled in case your pills are lost or run out), you should bring medicines against such common diseases as headaches, diarrhea, constipation, insect bites, sore throats, eye drops, cuts, etc. Medical standards outside Hanoi, Saigon, Siemriep and Luangprabang are lower than those found in western countries.

18. What is the local currency called? Can I use credit cards and travelers cheques?

Currency
  • Vietnamese - Dong
  • Cambodian- Riel
  • Laos - Kip

Credit Cards
  • In Viet Nam, credit cards are accepted at most hotels and better restaurants & shops. In Cambodia and Laos, credit cards are accepted in major hotels.
Travelers Cheques
  • In Viet Nam, Cambodia and Laos, travelers' checks are accepted on a limited basis and may be difficult to change. In Myanmar, they are generally unaccepted.
ATM Machines
  • In Viet Nam, ATM Machines are available in Hanoi, in Ho Chi Minh City and in major cities such as Haiphong, Halong, Hue, Hoian, Nhatrang, Vungtau, and Dalat. In Cambodia and Laos, ATM Machines are presently unavailable. US dollars, preferably intact & clean bills, are accepted almost everywhere in all of the countries listed above.
19. How can I apply for a visa?
Visitors must obtain visa approval prior to their entrance into a foreign country. We offer the visa approval service in order to facilitate your process of applying for a visa. The procedure can be summarized as follows:

   1.Passport details and arrival & departure dates are sent to us no less than 14 days prior to your departure. (You can send them via email, fax or snail mail. We need such information as: your full name as stated in your passport; your gender; your date of birth, your passport number and its expiry date, your nationality, your arrival & departure dates, the place to receive your visa, and at which Vietnam Embassy or Consulate your want to get your visa stamped)
   2.We then go ahead and submit these details to the Vietnamese Immigration Department.
   3.The Immigration Department will issue visa approval for us and send it via fax/telex to the Vietnam Embassy or Consulate in the visitors' country.
   4.We will send you the approval document via email.
   5.You need to bring your passports & 2 passport-sized photos to the Vietnam Embassy in your country to get the visa stamped (the stamp fee will be paid by yourselves).
