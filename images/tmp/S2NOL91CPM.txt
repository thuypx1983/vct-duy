<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><u><strong>Day 11: HCM city - Cu Chi tunnels and Cao Dai Temples - HCM city (B/L)</strong></u></span></div>
<ul>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">This morning you will drive to the Cao Dai Temples in Tay Ninh to observe the midday service.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Following lunch we will drive to the Cu Chi Tunnels and then return to HCMC at around 6 pm.&nbsp;</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Tay Ninh, near the Cambodian border, is home to the unique Cao Dai sect, whose patron saints include Joan of Arc, Victor Hugo and Winston Churchill to name a few.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">The religion is a hybrid of Buddhism, Taoism, Confucianism and Christianity. The Cao Dai Temple at Tay Ninh has been described as a &quot;Walt Disney World of the East.&quot; The temple's exterior is decorated with multi-colored dragons of all shapes and sizes competing for space with a number of religious symbols.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Above the main entrance is the all-seeing holy eye, the symbol of the Cao Dai sect. The interior is just as engaging, as statues of Jesus Christ, Buddha and the Hindu god, Brahma, stand side by side.&nbsp;</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">The trip to Tay Ninh is combined with a visit to the Cu Chi Tunnels.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">At its height, the tunnel system stretched over 250 kilometers, from Saigon to the Cambodian border, and was three levels deep.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">The network included innumerable trap doors, specially constructed living areas, storage facilities, weapons factories, field hospitals, command centers and kitchens.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Today the remaining tunnels have become a major tourist attraction giving the visitor a unique experience and a feel of what underground life during the U.S.-Vietnam War must have been like.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Dinner on your own.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Overnight in HCMC.</li>
</ul>
