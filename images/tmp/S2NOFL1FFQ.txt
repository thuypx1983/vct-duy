<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><u><strong>Day 1&nbsp; HO CHI MINH CITY / MY THO / BEN TRE / CAN THO&nbsp; (B/L)&nbsp; (175km &ndash; 4h30)</strong></u></span></div>
<ul>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Morning departure from Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon) and drive to My Tho, located in the heart of the Mekong Delta.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">When arriving in My Tho, take a boat for a cruise on the Mekong River and its multiple canals to Ben Tre.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Visit a coco candy factory where you can taste local specialties.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Take a 15-minute coach ride to visit a garden house to enjoy tea with honey and fresh tropical fruits.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Take a sampan through a water coconut forest before returning to the boat to come back to the restaurant for lunch with a chance to taste &ldquo;tai tuong&rdquo; fish, a special kind of fish having big ears.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">In the afternoon, continue to Can Tho, the main town of the Mekong Delta.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Dinner on your own.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Overnight in Can Tho.</li>
</ul>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><u><strong>Day 2&nbsp; CAN THO/ CAI RANG/ CHAU DOC&nbsp; (B/L)&nbsp; (120 km &ndash; 3h)</strong></u></span></div>
<ul>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">After breakfast, take a Sampan to visit Cai Rang floating market and a family running a noodle-making factory, discover the Ba Cong garden by tasting its fresh fruits.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Witness the rural life and the beautiful vegetation along the river banks.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Drive to Chau Doc, not far from the Cambodian border.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">On the road, visit a crocodile farm.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Check into your hotel.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Lunch in a local restaurant.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">In the afternoon, cruise through a surprising village of floating houses, where fishes are bred in their natural environment and learn more about Cham culture while visiting a small village and its mosque.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">End the day by visiting the Sam Mountain, a popular pilgrimage site including temples of Chinese, Cham or Khmer influences.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Dinner on your own.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Overnight in Chau Doc.</li>
</ul>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><u><strong>Day 3 CHAU DOC/ PHNOM PENH (B)</strong></u></span></div>
<ul>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">After an early breakfast, take a boat at 7h30 and cross the Cambodian border to reach Phnom Penh.</li>
    <li style="text-align: justify;">Arrive in Phnom Penh at mid-day.</li>
</ul>
